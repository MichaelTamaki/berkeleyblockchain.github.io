Who Are We?

We're a student-run organization at UC Berkeley dedicated to serving the Berkeley and greater East Bay crypto and blockchain communities. Our members include Berkeley students, alumni, community members, and blockchain enthusiasts from all educational and industrial backgrounds.

Our team consists of undergraduates from a variety of backgrounds: Electrical Engineering and Computer Science, Economics, Business, and more.

Learn More

What Do We Do?

We host a range of events and programs to promote blockchain in Berkeley and beyond, largely centered around education, consulting, and research and development.

Education

We teach two open-source undergraduate cryptocurrency courses, organize the largest crypto meetup in the East Bay, and host tech talks, workshops, and more.

Learn More

Consulting

We work with companies to explore how they can apply blockchain to their business, build Proof of Concepts or prototypes, and translate new developments into real-world use cases.

Learn More

Research &
Development

We build side projects and do research with cutting-edge blockchain and crypto technologies. These projects have won first prizes at collegiate hackathons. All of our work is open-sourced.
